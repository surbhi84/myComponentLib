    Badge

    A badge is a add-on to your element to convey a meaning or to pass an information that otherwise would have been rather difficult to explain.By using a badge you can pass on the information in a sleek and effortless manner without getting much into the details.A badge can be added to almost all types of elements but the most widely used are badges on buttons, avatars and icons. HalloUi provides you the two types, badges on icons and badges on avatars.

    Icons with badges

    A badge on icon can represent the amount of the information that the icon presents, for example,here 21 is the number of notifications that a user might have.you can use any icon of your choice and put it in the source the rest HalloUi will manage.HalloUi also enables you to choose a color of your choice to put in the background of badge. you can choose different colors from our color classes.
