Range Slider

Range sliders provides you a way of taking feedback in terms of ranges from the user and it can also be used for various filters or simply to communicate with the user.

Emoji Range Slider

HalloUi presents to you an emoji slider with easy to implement classes and you can choose any emojis of your choice. The custom emoji slider is suitable to take reviews or track moods of your fellows. It is a fun and entertaining way of taking feedback from your users. If you want to use different enojis for your slider just copy the desired emojis in place of the existing ones. Do take note that whatever width you will give to display-wrap or any other div you wanna wrap the slider in will take its parents full width. It is designed this way so that you can customise the length of the sliders.
